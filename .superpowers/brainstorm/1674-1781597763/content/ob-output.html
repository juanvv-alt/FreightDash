What should OB Forecast output?

You'll upload Oceanbolt tonnage CSVs per zone. What does the dashboard show you?

Mirror the existing Supply Forecast — directional signal per zone

Each of the 4 zones gets a BULLISH / BEARISH / NEUTRAL card with score + confidence, using the same z-score/regression logic as the AIS module. Consistent look & feel across the platform.

Tonnage trend charts — visual evolution, no signal

Charts showing the time series of each zone's tonnage count (vessel count + DWT), with week-over-week change highlighted. You read the trends yourself — no algorithmic signal.

Both — signal cards + trend charts underneath

Signal cards at the top (same as AIS Supply Forecast) plus expandable Chart.js time-series charts per zone below, showing all uploaded series (ballast/laden/in-port). Most information, most complex.
